## Why plumbers need a CRM

A CRM for plumbers is not a luxury, it is the difference between a business that grows and one that leaks revenue. Plumbing companies handle emergency calls, scheduled repairs, new installations, maintenance contracts, and estimates every single day, often while the owner is on-site with a wrench in hand. Without a system to manage it all, jobs fall through the cracks.

Here are the pain points that plumber CRM software solves:

- **Emergency calls go unanswered**: A burst pipe at one property, a blocked drain at another, and three missed calls in between. Without a central system, some customers never hear back, and they call your competitor instead.
- **Estimates get forgotten**: You visit a site, write up an estimate, and send it off. Two weeks later you realize you never followed up. The customer went with someone else.
- **Customer history disappears**: You fixed a boiler at that address two years ago, but you cannot remember the model or what you did. Repeat visits take longer and cost more than they should.
- **Maintenance contracts slip away**: Annual boiler services, seasonal drain inspections, warranty follow-ups, without automated reminders, this reliable recurring revenue vanishes.
- **Scheduling and dispatch is chaotic**: Who is going where tomorrow? What parts do they need? When dispatch lives on a whiteboard or in someone's head, double-bookings and missed appointments are inevitable.
- **No visibility into business performance**: How many open estimates are outstanding? What is your close rate? Which technician is overbooked? Without data, you are guessing.

A CRM (Customer Relationship Management) for plumbers is software that helps plumbing businesses manage customer information, track jobs from inquiry to invoice, schedule technicians, follow up on estimates, and automate recurring tasks like maintenance reminders. Instead of relying on memory, spreadsheets, or a whiteboard, a plumbing CRM keeps everything in one searchable system.
