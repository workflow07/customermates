## Why tradespeople need a CRM

A CRM for tradespeople replaces the scattered notes, missed callbacks, and spreadsheet chaos with a single system that tracks every lead, every quote, and every job. In the trades, business runs on personal relationships, referrals, and repeat clients — but as your workload grows, keeping everything in your head or on scraps of paper stops working.

Without a tradesperson CRM, common problems pile up fast:

- **Inquiries vanish**: A homeowner calls while you are on a roof. You scribble a number, but it disappears into a pocket or truck console.
- **Quotes go cold**: You send an estimate on Monday, get busy all week, and a competitor who followed up on Wednesday wins the job.
- **Client data lives everywhere**: Contacts in your phone, job notes in emails, invoices in a folder — nobody else on your team can find anything.
- **Repeat business slips away**: A customer you did great work for last year needs you again, but you have no system to stay in touch or remind yourself.
- **Scheduling collisions**: Two jobs booked for the same morning, or a site visit forgotten entirely because it was only in a text message.
- **No visibility into your pipeline**: You do not know how many quotes are pending, what your win rate is, or where next month's revenue is coming from.

A CRM for trade businesses brings structure to the business side of your work so you can focus on the trade itself. Customermates gives you [pipeline management](/features/pipeline), [contact tracking](/features/contact-management), and [task management](/features/task-management) — without enterprise complexity or cost.

## How Customermates works as a trades CRM

Customermates is built for trade businesses of every size — from a sole trader to a growing crew with office staff. Here is how it supports every stage of a job.

### Job pipeline from inquiry to invoice

Track every opportunity from first call through site visit, quote, acceptance, job completion, and final invoice. The [Kanban pipeline view](/features/pipeline) lets you drag jobs between stages so you always see where every deal stands. Set follow-up dates on quotes so no estimate sits unanswered. Whether you are a [plumber](/for/plumbers), [electrician](/for/electricians), or [HVAC technician](/for/hvac), the pipeline adapts to your workflow.

### Quote management and follow-up

Sending quotes is only half the battle — winning the work requires timely follow-up. Log every quote against the client record, set a reminder for 3 or 5 days later, and see at a glance which estimates need chasing. Track quote values so you know how much revenue is pending and where to focus your effort.

### Customer records with full history

Every client gets a complete profile: contact details, property address, all past jobs, emails, notes, and files. When a repeat customer calls, you know exactly what you did last time, when you did it, and what they paid. Build lasting relationships with proper records instead of relying on memory. Manage all of this from [contact management](/features/contact-management).

### Scheduling and task assignment

See upcoming site visits, jobs, and follow-ups in one place. Assign [tasks](/features/task-management) to team members with due dates and notes — who is visiting the client tomorrow, what materials need ordering, which inspection is due this week. Avoid double-booking and keep your crew coordinated without back-and-forth phone calls.

### Invoicing workflow integration

Customermates tracks jobs through to completion and connects to your invoicing tools via [n8n workflow automation](/features/workflow-automation). When a job moves to "Completed," trigger an automatic notification to generate the invoice, send a payment reminder, or request a review. Keep revenue flowing without manual follow-up.

### Repeat business tracking

Your most profitable work comes from returning clients and their referrals. Customermates helps you track annual maintenance visits, seasonal check-ins, and follow-up reminders. Tag clients for recurring services — boiler servicing, electrical inspections, annual gutter cleaning — and let automations remind you when it is time to reach out.

### Automation with n8n

Use built-in [n8n workflow automation](/features/workflow-automation) to eliminate repetitive admin:

- Send automatic follow-up emails 3 days after submitting a quote
- Trigger a satisfaction survey when a job is marked complete
- Send annual service reminders to past clients
- Notify your team when a deal moves to "Accepted"
- Request referrals and reviews from happy customers
- Create a task when a repeat client's maintenance window opens

### AI agents for tradespeople

Use AI to draft professional follow-up emails, summarize long client email threads, enrich new leads with property details, or analyze your win rate across different job types and seasons. AI agents handle the admin so you can stay on the tools.
