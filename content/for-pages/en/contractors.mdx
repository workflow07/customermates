## Why contractors need a CRM

Running a contracting business means juggling estimates, job sites, subcontractors, and client expectations, often from the cab of your truck. A CRM for contractors replaces the scattered notes, text messages, and spreadsheets with a single system that tracks every lead, every quote, and every job from start to finish.

Without a contractor CRM, common problems stack up fast:

- **Estimates go cold**: You sent a quote last week. The homeowner never responded, and without a follow-up reminder the job goes to a competitor who chased it.
- **Leads disappear**: A referral calls while you are on-site. You jot down a number, but it gets buried in your phone or truck console.
- **Repeat clients are forgotten**: A customer you did excellent work for two years ago needs you again, but you have no system to stay in touch or track their history.
- **Subcontractor coordination falls apart**: Managing availability, rates, and contact details for your regular subs adds complexity that spreadsheets cannot handle.
- **No visibility into your pipeline**: You do not know how many estimates are pending, what your close rate is, or where your next month of revenue is coming from.

A construction contractor CRM brings structure to the business side of contracting so you can focus on the work itself. Customermates gives you exactly the tools you need, [pipeline management](/features/pipeline), [contact tracking](/features/contact-management), and [workflow automation](/features/workflow-automation), without enterprise-level complexity or cost.

Every email, note, and call log is recorded against the client record. When a past customer calls back, you know exactly what work you did, when, and how it went. [Email integration](/features/email-integration) syncs your inbox so conversations are captured automatically, no manual data entry from the job site.

### Scheduling and workload visibility

See your upcoming jobs, site visits, and follow-ups in one view. Avoid double-booking crews or missing estimate appointments. Custom fields let you track job dates, crew assignments, and milestones without switching between apps.
