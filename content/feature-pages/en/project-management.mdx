## What Is CRM Project Management?

CRM project management is the practice of managing tasks, milestones, and team collaboration directly within your customer relationship management platform. Instead of switching between a CRM for sales and a separate tool like Monday, Asana, or Trello for project tracking, a CRM with built-in project management keeps everything connected in one system.

For many businesses, the handoff from sales to delivery is where things break down. A deal closes, but the tasks needed to fulfill that commitment live in a different system. Context gets lost, deadlines slip, and customers notice the disconnect. When project management and CRM share the same platform, that handoff becomes seamless. The deal record, customer history, communication logs, and project tasks all exist in one place.

Beyond the sales-to-delivery transition, CRM project management is valuable for managing internal sales operations, campaign coordination, proposal workflows, onboarding sequences, and account reviews. Any process that involves multiple steps, multiple people, and customer context benefits from project management inside the CRM.

## How Project Management Works in Customermates

Customermates includes a comprehensive project management system that integrates naturally with your contacts, deals, and organizations. Every feature described below is included at EUR 10 per user per month.

**Task creation and assignment.** Create tasks directly from any deal, contact, or organization record. Assign tasks to specific team members with due dates, priority levels, and descriptions. Tasks appear on the assignee's personal task list and on the linked record, ensuring nothing gets missed from either perspective.

**Deal-linked project tasks.** Every task can be linked to a deal record, creating a direct connection between project work and revenue. When you open a deal, you see all associated tasks, their status, and who is responsible. This gives you a complete picture of what needs to happen to move a deal forward or deliver on a closed commitment.

**Milestones and checkpoints.** Define milestones within your project workflows to mark key delivery points. Milestones provide visibility into overall project health and help teams understand whether they are on track. Use milestones in combination with n8n automation to trigger notifications or escalations when deadlines approach.

**Task dependencies and sequencing.** Structure tasks in logical sequences where certain tasks must be completed before others can begin. This prevents teams from working out of order and ensures that prerequisite steps are completed before downstream work starts.

**Kanban board view.** Visualize project tasks on a kanban board organized by status, priority, assignee, or custom groupings. Drag tasks between columns to update progress. The kanban view is ideal for stand-up meetings and provides a clear picture of project flow and bottlenecks.

**Task status tracking and progress dashboards.** Track task progress with customizable status labels. Mark tasks as open, in progress, in review, or completed. Filter your task list by status, assignee, due date, or linked record. Progress dashboards show project completion percentages, overdue counts, and workload distribution at a glance.

**Due date management and overdue detection.** Set due dates on every task and monitor overdue items across the team. Overdue tasks are clearly flagged with visual indicators, making it easy for managers to spot bottlenecks and for individuals to prioritize their workload.

**Notes, comments, and file attachments.** Add notes to tasks as work progresses. Log activity, attach files, and maintain a history of what was done and when. Task-level comments keep the conversation about specific work items separate from the broader deal or contact record.

**Project templates.** Save commonly used project structures as templates. When you start a new onboarding, proposal process, or campaign, apply a template to instantly generate the full set of tasks with pre-defined assignees, due date offsets, and descriptions. Templates ensure consistency and save setup time.

**Automation with n8n.** Connect project management to automated workflows using the built-in n8n engine. Automatically create project task sets when a deal enters a new stage. Send reminders when tasks approach their due date. Notify managers when tasks are overdue. Trigger external systems when milestones are reached. n8n lets you build project workflows that match your actual business processes.

## Common Project Management Use Cases

**Post-sale onboarding.** When a deal is won, automatically generate an onboarding project: schedule a kickoff call, send welcome materials, configure the customer account, assign a customer success manager, and set a 30-day check-in milestone. Every new customer gets a consistent onboarding experience with clear accountability.

**Proposal preparation.** Creating a proposal involves multiple steps and team members. Apply a proposal template that generates tasks for gathering requirements, drafting the proposal, internal review, revision, legal approval, and delivery. Link the entire sequence to the deal so progress is visible to everyone.

**Quarterly account reviews.** Schedule recurring project templates for account reviews with key customers. Each template includes preparation tasks, the review meeting itself, and follow-up actions with assigned owners and deadlines. Never miss a quarterly touchpoint with your most important accounts.

**Marketing campaign execution.** Plan and execute marketing campaigns with project task lists that track content creation, design, review, scheduling, distribution, and performance analysis. Link campaign tasks to the contacts and deals they generate for full attribution.

**Customer implementation projects.** For products or services that require implementation, manage the entire process inside the CRM. Technical setup, data migration, user training, and go-live milestones are all tracked alongside the customer record.

**Internal process management.** Use projects to manage internal processes like hiring workflows, training programs, office moves, or process improvement initiatives. Project management in Customermates is not limited to customer-facing work.
