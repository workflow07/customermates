## What Is CRM Outlook Integration?

CRM Outlook integration connects Microsoft Outlook with your customer relationship management system, creating a seamless bridge between your email inbox, calendar, and CRM data. It allows your team to manage customer relationships from within Outlook while ensuring that every communication is captured in the CRM for the entire team to access.

Email remains the primary B2B communication channel. Your sales team sends dozens of emails to prospects and customers every day through Outlook. Without CRM integration, these conversations exist only in individual inboxes, invisible to the rest of the team and disconnected from the deal and contact records where they belong. When someone is out sick, on vacation, or leaves the company, their email context leaves with them.

Outlook integration eliminates this problem. Emails are automatically logged to the corresponding CRM records. Calendar events are synchronized. Contacts stay consistent between both systems. Your team works in Outlook as they always have, while the CRM captures everything in the background.

## How Outlook Integration Works in Customermates

Customermates integrates with Microsoft Outlook 365 through native email connectivity and the n8n automation engine, providing multiple options for syncing email, calendar, and contact data.

### Setup in Three Steps

**Step 1: Connect your Outlook account.** Navigate to Settings in Customermates, select Email Integration, and sign in with your Microsoft 365 credentials using secure OAuth authentication. No IT department involvement needed, no Outlook plugins to install.

**Step 2: Configure your sync preferences.** Choose which emails to sync: all emails, only emails matching existing CRM contacts, or specific folders. Set calendar sync preferences and contact sync direction. You control exactly what data flows between Outlook and your CRM.

**Step 3: Start working.** Emails begin syncing immediately. Every email you send to or receive from a CRM contact appears on their timeline. Calendar events with CRM contacts show up on their activity record. Your team works in Outlook as usual while the CRM organizes everything automatically.

### Email Synchronization

Bidirectional email sync captures both sent and received emails. When you send an email to a prospect from Outlook, it appears on their CRM timeline within seconds. When they reply, the response is logged automatically. The complete email thread lives on the CRM record for your entire team to see, not just the person who sent it.

Customermates matches emails by checking sender and recipient addresses against your contact database. Matching is automatic and accurate. If the sender is not yet in your CRM, you can create a new contact directly from the email with one click.

### Calendar Synchronization

Your Outlook calendar stays in sync with CRM activities. When you schedule a meeting in Outlook with a CRM contact, the meeting appears on the contact's activity timeline. When you create an activity in the CRM with a date and time, it appears in your Outlook calendar. Bidirectional sync means your team never has to update both systems manually.

Meeting history becomes part of the CRM record. Managers can see meeting frequency and recency for every contact, helping them understand relationship health and identify accounts that need attention.

### Contact Synchronization

Keep your Outlook contacts consistent with your CRM contacts. When contact details are updated in Customermates (new phone number, job title change, address update), the changes can sync to Outlook automatically. When you add a new contact in Outlook, it can be created in the CRM as well. One update applies everywhere.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Does it work with Outlook desktop or only Outlook 365?**
Customermates integrates with Microsoft Outlook 365 (Microsoft 365) through server-side sync. This covers Outlook on the web, Outlook desktop connected to a Microsoft 365 account, and the Outlook mobile app. On-premise Exchange servers are supported through IMAP configuration.

**Do I need to install an Outlook plugin?**
No. The integration works through server-side OAuth authentication. There is nothing to install in Outlook, no browser extensions, and no add-ins that need maintenance.

**Can I choose which emails are synced?**
Yes. You can sync all emails, only emails matching existing CRM contacts, or specific Outlook folders. You have full control over which communications enter the CRM.

**Is calendar sync bidirectional?**
Yes. Meetings created in Outlook appear on CRM timelines, and activities created in the CRM with dates appear in your Outlook calendar.

**Can multiple team members connect their Outlook accounts?**
Yes. Each team member connects their own Outlook account. Emails from all connected accounts are logged on the shared CRM contact records, giving the entire team visibility into all customer communications.

**Is my email data stored in the EU?**
Yes. All synchronized email data is stored in European data centers with full GDPR compliance. When you self-host, data stays on your own servers.
