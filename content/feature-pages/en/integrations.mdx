## Why CRM Integrations Matter

CRM integrations connect your customer relationship management system to the other tools your business depends on. They let data flow between platforms, trigger actions across systems, and keep information synchronized without manual copy-paste work.

No CRM operates in isolation. Your sales team uses [email](/features/email-integration), [Slack](/features/slack-integration), [Outlook](/features/outlook-integration), accounting software, marketing platforms, and project management tools daily. Without integrations, data lives in silos, team members waste hours on duplicate data entry, and information becomes inconsistent across tools. CRM integrations eliminate these problems by turning your CRM into the connected hub of your business operations.

The average SMB uses 20 to 40 software tools. Each disconnected tool creates friction, delays, and errors. A well-integrated CRM becomes the single source of truth that ties everything together, and the value compounds with every new connection you add.

## How CRM Integrations Work in Customermates

Customermates gives you three complementary integration approaches so you can connect any tool regardless of your technical skill level.

### n8n Automation Engine (Built In)

Customermates includes [n8n](https://n8n.io), the leading open-source workflow automation platform, directly embedded in the CRM. n8n provides a visual, drag-and-drop workflow builder with pre-built connectors for over 400 services including:

- **Communication:** Slack, Microsoft Teams, Discord, Telegram
- **Email:** Gmail, Outlook, SMTP, Mailchimp, SendGrid
- **Productivity:** Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, Notion, Airtable
- **Accounting:** Xero, QuickBooks, lexoffice, sevDesk
- **Marketing:** HubSpot Marketing, ActiveCampaign, Brevo
- **Development:** GitHub, GitLab, Jira, Linear
- **Storage:** Google Drive, Dropbox, OneDrive, S3

Build integrations visually by connecting nodes on a canvas. No code required. Each workflow can include conditional logic, data transformation, error handling, and scheduling.

### REST API

For developers and teams that need programmatic access, Customermates provides a comprehensive REST API. Access and manipulate contacts, deals, organizations, tasks, and all other CRM data through standard HTTP requests. The API supports full CRUD operations, filtering, pagination, bulk operations, and authentication via API keys. When you self-host Customermates, there are no artificial rate limits.

### Webhooks

Receive real-time push notifications when events happen in your CRM. Configure webhooks to fire when contacts are created, deals change stage, tasks are completed, or any other CRM event occurs. Webhooks push data to your systems instantly, enabling event-driven architectures without polling.

## Popular CRM Integration Scenarios

**CRM to [Slack](/features/slack-integration) notifications.** Send deal updates, new lead alerts, and task reminders to Slack channels automatically. A three-node n8n workflow connects the CRM trigger to a Slack message node, and your team gets real-time updates in the channel they already use.

**[Email](/features/email-integration) marketing synchronization.** Sync contacts and segments from Customermates to Mailchimp, Brevo, or ActiveCampaign. When a contact's data changes in the CRM, the marketing platform updates automatically. When a lead fills out a form, the CRM creates the contact and adds them to the correct email list.

**Accounting integration.** When a deal is marked as won, automatically create an invoice in lexoffice, sevDesk, Xero, or QuickBooks. Sync customer and payment data between your CRM and financial systems without manual data transfer.

**Calendar synchronization.** Sync CRM activities with Google Calendar or [Outlook](/features/outlook-integration). Meetings, follow-up reminders, and task deadlines appear in your calendar without manual entry, and calendar events create CRM activities automatically.

**Web form lead capture.** When a prospect fills out a form on your website, n8n creates a contact in Customermates, assigns the lead to the right sales representative based on form data, and triggers a welcome email. The entire flow runs in seconds with zero manual steps.

**Project management handoff.** When a deal closes, automatically create a project in Asana, Monday, or Notion with relevant deal details pre-filled. The transition from sales to delivery happens without anyone copying data between systems.

**[LinkedIn](/features/linkedin-integration) contact enrichment.** When a new contact is added to the CRM, look up their LinkedIn profile and pull in job title, company, and location. Enriched contacts give your team better context for every conversation.
