## What Is CRM Email Integration?

CRM email integration connects your email client to your customer relationship management system so that every message you send and receive is automatically associated with the right contact record. It eliminates the manual process of logging communications and ensures your entire team has visibility into customer conversations.

Without email integration, critical information is trapped in individual inboxes. When a colleague is out of office or a deal gets reassigned, the new owner lacks context. They have to ask for forwarded threads, search through shared mailboxes, or start conversations over from scratch. Email integration solves this by making communication history a shared, searchable resource attached to every contact and deal record.

For sales and customer success teams, email integration is the single highest-impact CRM feature. It bridges the gap between where communication happens (your inbox) and where relationship data lives (your CRM), without changing how your team works.

## How Email Integration Works in Customermates

Customermates provides native email integration that connects your existing email accounts to your CRM. Setup takes under two minutes and requires no IT involvement.

### Step 1: Connect Your Email Account

Navigate to Settings, select Email Integration, and connect your Gmail or Microsoft [Outlook](/features/outlook-integration) account with OAuth authentication. Customermates supports Gmail, Outlook 365, and any IMAP-compatible email provider. Your credentials are encrypted and stored in EU data centers.

### Step 2: Configure Sync Rules

Choose what gets synced. Options include syncing all emails, only emails matching existing CRM contacts, or only emails you manually associate. You control the scope so only relevant communications enter the CRM.

### Step 3: Start Working

Once connected, emails sync automatically. Every incoming and outgoing email matching a CRM contact appears on their timeline. Your team works in their inbox as usual while the CRM captures everything in the background.

## Core Email Integration Features

**Gmail and Outlook sync.** Bidirectional email synchronization with Gmail, Outlook 365, and IMAP providers. Emails are matched to contact records based on email addresses. New emails appear on CRM timelines within seconds of being sent or received.

**Automatic contact matching.** When an email arrives, Customermates checks sender and recipient addresses against your contact database. Matching emails are linked to the correct record automatically. If the sender is not in your CRM, you can create a new contact directly from the email with one click.

**Email timeline on every record.** Every contact, organization, and deal record includes a complete email timeline showing all correspondence. Emails are displayed chronologically alongside notes, tasks, calls, and deal updates. One screen gives you the full picture of every interaction.

**Reusable email templates.** Create templates for introductions, follow-ups, proposals, meeting requests, and any other recurring message. Templates support dynamic variables that auto-fill with contact data: first name, company name, deal value, and custom fields. A template that sends in 30 seconds would take five minutes to compose manually. Across 20 emails per day, that saves over 1.5 hours daily per sales representative.

**Send emails from the CRM.** Compose and send emails directly from within Customermates. Sent emails are automatically logged on the contact record and replies flow back into the same thread. Your team handles email without switching between applications.

**Email open and click tracking.** Know when your emails are opened and which links are clicked. Tracking data appears on the contact timeline and helps your team prioritize follow-ups with prospects who are actively engaging. Open and click rates are aggregated in [reporting dashboards](/features/reporting) so you can measure email effectiveness across the team.

**Scheduled email sending.** Write emails now, send them later. Schedule emails to arrive at the optimal time for your recipient's time zone. Scheduled sending is built into the CRM composer, no third-party tools needed.

**Bulk email sending.** Send personalized emails to groups of contacts using templates and dynamic variables. Filter your contact list, select recipients, choose a template, and send. Each email is personalized and logged individually on the recipient's record.

**Automated email sequences with n8n.** Go beyond manual email with the built-in n8n automation engine. Set up triggers that send emails automatically based on CRM events:

- When a deal moves to "Proposal Sent," send a follow-up template after 3 days if no reply
- When a new contact is created from a web form, trigger a welcome email sequence
- When a task is overdue, notify the responsible team member by email
- When a deal is marked as won, send a thank-you email to the customer

Build these workflows visually in n8n with no coding required. Sequences that would require a dedicated email marketing tool like Mailchimp or a sales engagement platform run directly inside your CRM.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Which email providers are supported?**
Customermates supports Gmail, Microsoft Outlook 365, and any email provider that supports IMAP. This covers the vast majority of business email setups including Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, and self-hosted email servers.

**Is email tracking included?**
Yes. Email open and click tracking is included in the standard EUR 10 per user per month price. There is no separate tracking add-on or premium tier required.

**How quickly do emails sync?**
Emails typically appear on CRM contact timelines within seconds of being sent or received. Synchronization runs continuously, not on a delayed schedule.

**Can I send automated email sequences?**
Yes. The built-in n8n automation engine lets you build multi-step email sequences triggered by CRM events. Sequences can include delays, conditions, and branching logic, all configured visually without code.

**Does email integration work with shared mailboxes?**
Yes. You can connect shared mailboxes so that emails sent to a team address are logged on the appropriate contact records. Multiple team members can access the same email thread from the CRM.

**Is my email data stored in the EU?**
Yes. All email synchronization data is stored in European data centers with full GDPR compliance. When you self-host Customermates, email data is stored on your own servers in whatever jurisdiction you choose.
