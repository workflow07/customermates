## What Is CRM LinkedIn Integration?

CRM LinkedIn integration connects your customer relationship management system with LinkedIn to sync contact data, enrich CRM records with professional profile information, track social selling activities, and manage lead generation workflows. It bridges the gap between your professional network on LinkedIn and the structured customer data in your CRM.

LinkedIn is the primary platform where B2B relationships develop. Your prospects, customers, and partners maintain LinkedIn profiles containing valuable professional data: job titles, company affiliations, career histories, mutual connections, industry information, and engagement activity. Without integration, this information exists in a separate silo from your CRM, requiring manual lookup and duplicated data entry.

When LinkedIn and your CRM are connected, your sales team works more efficiently. They see LinkedIn context alongside deal and contact data, can enrich CRM records with up-to-date professional information, track social selling activities as part of their overall pipeline, and receive alerts when key contacts change roles or companies.

## Social Selling Workflows That Drive Revenue

**New connection to CRM pipeline.** When you connect with a prospect on LinkedIn, automatically create a contact and deal in Customermates. Assign the lead to a nurture pipeline, set a follow-up task, and notify the account owner. Every connection becomes a tracked opportunity.

**Pre-call LinkedIn research.** Before a sales call, pull up-to-date LinkedIn data for the contact and their organization. Current job title, recent posts, mutual connections, shared groups, and company news give your team relevant talking points and personalization signals.

**Job change outreach trigger.** When a champion at a customer account moves to a new company, trigger an automated outreach sequence. Alert the account manager, create a new organization record for the target company, and generate a task to reach out within 48 hours. Job changes are one of the highest-converting sales triggers in B2B.

**Content engagement scoring.** Track which prospects and customers engage with your LinkedIn content. Log engagement as activities on CRM records and factor it into lead scoring. Contacts who engage with your content are warmer leads who are more likely to respond to outreach.

**Multi-channel outreach sequences.** Build outreach sequences that combine LinkedIn touchpoints with email and phone. Track the entire sequence in the CRM with each LinkedIn interaction logged as an activity. See which channels drive the most responses and optimize your sequence accordingly.

**Account-based marketing coordination.** Combine CRM account data with LinkedIn company insights to prioritize target accounts. Sync target account lists to LinkedIn for matched audience advertising. Track LinkedIn engagement at the account level alongside deal progress for full ABM visibility.
