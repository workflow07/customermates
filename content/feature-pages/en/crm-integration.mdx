## What Is CRM Integration?

CRM integration is the process of connecting your customer relationship management system with the other software tools and platforms your business depends on. This includes [email clients](/features/email-integration), calendars, accounting software, marketing platforms, support desks, communication tools like [Slack](/features/slack-integration), and custom internal systems.

The goal is straightforward: eliminate data silos. When your CRM is disconnected from your other tools, your team wastes time copying data between systems, information becomes inconsistent, and context gets lost. Integrated systems share data automatically, keep every tool in sync, and give your team a single source of truth for customer information.

Effective CRM integration turns your CRM from a standalone contact database into the connected hub of your entire business operation. Data flows in from lead forms, email, and phone calls. It flows out to invoicing, project management, and marketing automation. Every system stays aligned without manual intervention.

### Built-in n8n Automation (Replaces Zapier)

This is where Customermates stands apart from other CRMs. The integrated [n8n](https://n8n.io) automation engine provides a visual workflow builder with 400+ pre-built connectors. Connect your CRM to Slack, Google Sheets, Mailchimp, Stripe, Notion, Airtable, Xero, lexoffice, and hundreds more without writing a single line of code.

n8n serves as a powerful open-source alternative to Zapier or Make, but it runs directly inside your CRM. No separate subscription, no per-execution fees, and no data leaving your environment. Build multi-step workflows with conditional logic, data transformation, error handling, and scheduling, all through a visual drag-and-drop interface.

**Example workflow:** When a deal moves to "Closed Won" in Customermates, n8n automatically creates an invoice in lexoffice, sends a congratulations message to the Slack sales channel, creates a project in Notion with the deal details, and adds the customer to a post-sale email sequence in Brevo. Five systems updated from one CRM event, zero manual work.

### REST API

For developers and custom applications, Customermates exposes a comprehensive REST API covering all CRM entities: contacts, organizations, deals, tasks, notes, and custom fields. Full CRUD operations, filtering, pagination, bulk operations, and API key authentication. Documentation is thorough with clear endpoints and response formats. When you self-host, there are no artificial rate limits.

### Webhooks

Configure webhooks to push real-time notifications when events occur in your CRM. Deal created, contact updated, task completed, stage changed, or any custom event triggers an HTTP POST to the URL you specify. Webhooks enable event-driven architectures where your systems react instantly to CRM changes without polling.

## Popular CRM Integration Use Cases

**CRM + [Email](/features/email-integration).** Sync Gmail and [Outlook](/features/outlook-integration) with your CRM. Every sent and received email is automatically logged on the contact record. Templates, tracking, and automated sequences are included.

**CRM + [Slack](/features/slack-integration).** Send deal updates, new lead alerts, and task reminders to Slack channels. Create CRM records from Slack messages. Keep your team informed in the communication tool they already use.

**CRM + Accounting.** When a deal closes, automatically create an invoice in lexoffice, sevDesk, Xero, or QuickBooks. Sync customer data, payment status, and revenue figures between your CRM and financial systems.

**CRM + Marketing Automation.** Sync contacts and segments to Mailchimp, Brevo, or ActiveCampaign. Trigger email campaigns based on deal stages, contact fields, or custom events. Measure campaign results alongside CRM data.

**CRM + Web Forms.** Capture leads from website forms and create contacts automatically. Route leads to the right sales representative based on form data, geography, or deal value. Trigger welcome sequences instantly.

**CRM + Calendar.** Sync activities with Google Calendar or Outlook. Meeting scheduling, follow-up reminders, and task deadlines flow between your CRM and calendar in both directions.

**CRM + Project Management.** When a deal closes, create a project in Asana, Monday, Notion, or Linear with deal details pre-filled. The sales-to-delivery handoff happens automatically.

**CRM + [LinkedIn](/features/linkedin-integration).** Enrich contact records with LinkedIn profile data. Import connections, track social selling activities, and monitor job changes for key contacts.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How is CRM integration different from CRM integrations?**
"CRM integration" refers to the general process and strategy of connecting your CRM to other tools. "[CRM integrations](/features/integrations)" refers to the specific connections and connectors available. Customermates supports both: a robust integration architecture (API, webhooks, n8n) and 400+ specific pre-built integrations.

**Can Customermates replace Zapier?**
For CRM-related automations, yes. The built-in n8n engine provides equivalent functionality including multi-step workflows, conditional logic, data transformation, and scheduling. Teams that only use Zapier for CRM connections can eliminate their Zapier subscription entirely.

**Do I need technical skills to set up integrations?**
No. The n8n visual workflow builder lets anyone build integrations by dragging and connecting nodes. For standard scenarios like Slack notifications, email sync, or calendar integration, no coding is required. The API and webhooks are available for teams with development resources who need custom solutions.

**Is there a limit to how many integrations I can create?**
No. You can create as many n8n workflows, API connections, and webhook endpoints as you need. There are no per-integration fees and no artificial limits on the number of active integrations.

**What happens to my integrations if I self-host?**
When you self-host Customermates, the n8n engine runs on your infrastructure alongside the CRM. All workflow data, API credentials, and execution logs stay on your servers. You have complete control over your integration environment.
