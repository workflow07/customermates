## At a glance

Customermates can run in two ways:

- Cloud: managed by Customermates
- Self-hosted: run by your team in your own infrastructure

This page helps teams compare managed and self-hosted CRM deployment models without reducing the decision to a single feature.
The best option depends on your operational model, privacy requirements, and how much infrastructure ownership you want.

## How to decide

Choose cloud if you want to minimize operational work and get to production quickly.

Choose self-hosted if your team prefers infrastructure ownership and is comfortable maintaining updates and runtime operations.

Both models support API usage, integrations, and automation workflows. Paid Enterprise features (Audit Logging, Single Sign-On, Managed AI Agent, Whitelabeling) are available only in cloud deployments by default.
If you want to deploy on your own server, continue with [Self-Hosting Get Started](/docs/self-hosting) and [Managing Your Self-Hosted Installation](/docs/managing-your-installation).
