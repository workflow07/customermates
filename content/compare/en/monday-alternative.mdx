> Monday.com is a well-known project management and work OS platform that has expanded into CRM territory with its Monday Sales CRM product. While it benefits from a familiar interface and strong project management roots, CRM functionality is essentially a bolt-on rather than a core competency. Customermates, by contrast, is a purpose-built, open-source CRM from the ground up, with contacts, deals, pipelines, AI agents, and n8n automation included at a flat price of €10 per user per month.

## Setup & Onboarding

Customermates lets you get started in two minutes. Sign up, configure your pipelines, and begin working with real data immediately. The interface is built specifically for CRM workflows, so everything feels intuitive from day one.

Monday.com requires more setup time because you are essentially configuring a project management tool to behave like a CRM. You need to set up boards, create custom columns for deal stages, and manually build the workflows that a dedicated CRM provides out of the box. The CRM add-on helps, but it still carries the complexity of the underlying Work OS platform.

## Pricing & Cost Model

Customermates offers a single, transparent price: €10 per user per month. All features included, no tier upgrades, no hidden limits.

Monday.com CRM starts at €12 per seat per month (Basic) but requires a minimum of 3 seats, so you are paying at least €36/month from the start. The Standard plan at €17/seat and Pro plan at €28/seat add features that many CRM users consider essential, such as advanced automations, integrations, and email tracking. Automation actions are capped per plan, the Standard plan includes 250 actions per month, and exceeding them requires an upgrade. Yearly billing offers an 18% discount, but the minimum seat requirement remains.

For a team of 10 on the Pro plan, Monday.com costs €280/month. The same team on Customermates pays €100/month with every feature included and no automation caps.

## Open-Source & Transparency

Customermates is open-source. You can inspect the code, self-host the platform, and maintain full control over your data. There is no vendor lock-in, and you own your infrastructure.

Monday.com is proprietary software. Your data lives on their US-based infrastructure, customization is limited to what the platform permits, and migrating away means rebuilding your CRM workflows from scratch.

## Features & CRM Capabilities

This is where the fundamental difference becomes clear. Customermates is built as a CRM: contacts, organizations, deals, pipelines, tasks, services, email integration, AI agents, and n8n automation are all native features. Every feature is available at one price point.

Monday.com is built as a project management platform. CRM capabilities were added later as an overlay. While you can track deals and contacts, the experience often feels like you are working within a spreadsheet or board structure rather than a dedicated CRM. Features like deal tracking, pipeline management, and contact enrichment are less mature compared to purpose-built CRM solutions. The CRM product depends on the Work OS foundation, which means you carry project management complexity even when you only need sales tools.

## AI Agents & Automation

Customermates includes AI agents natively, along with full n8n automation. Build intelligent workflows from day one, automate follow-ups, qualify leads, enrich contact data, all without plan-based restrictions.

Monday.com offers AI features and automations, but they are capped by plan. The Basic plan includes limited automation runs per month, and AI capabilities are progressively unlocked at higher tiers. Exceeding automation caps requires upgrading to a more expensive plan, making costs unpredictable as your usage grows.

## Data Protection & Hosting

Customermates is GDPR-native. All data is hosted exclusively in the EU (Germany), and you retain full control over your data. Self-hosting is also available for organizations with strict compliance requirements.

Monday.com is a US-based company with data hosted primarily in the United States. While they offer some compliance features, data sovereignty and EU-only hosting are not guaranteed. For European companies with strict data protection requirements, this can be a significant concern.

## Integrations

Customermates includes n8n automation built in, along with full API access. Connect to thousands of services without plan-based restrictions or additional costs.

Monday.com offers a marketplace of integrations, but many of the more advanced capabilities are locked behind higher-tier plans. Integration actions, like automations, are capped monthly, adding another layer of cost management as your workflows scale.

## Conclusion

Monday.com is an excellent project management tool, but using it as your primary CRM means accepting compromises. CRM features are secondary to its core project management identity, pricing scales quickly with seats and plan tiers, automations are capped, and data remains US-hosted. Customermates is purpose-built for CRM workflows, open-source, offers all features at a flat €10/user/month, includes AI agents and n8n automation without caps, and keeps your data in the EU. For teams that want a real CRM without the overhead of a project management platform, Customermates is the clear choice.
