## Why Teams Look for a HubSpot Alternative

[HubSpot](https://www.hubspot.com/) dominates the CRM market with a reported 35%+ market share in marketing automation. It started as an inbound marketing tool and has grown into an enterprise platform spanning sales, marketing, service, and content management. That breadth comes with serious trade-offs.

The most common reasons teams leave HubSpot are escalating costs, US-centric data handling, and platform complexity that requires dedicated administrators. According to [G2 reviews](https://www.g2.com/products/hubspot-crm/reviews), the most frequent complaints center on pricing surprises when scaling and the difficulty of configuring advanced features without consulting help.

For small and mid-sized businesses that need a CRM that works from day one — without a six-figure annual contract — a focused HubSpot alternative makes more sense.

## Pricing: Flat vs. Tiered

The pricing contrast between Customermates and HubSpot is the most striking difference. Customermates charges **€10 per user per month** — flat, with every feature included. No tiered pricing, no feature gates, no usage caps. Check the [pricing page](/pricing) for full details.

HubSpot uses a complex hub-and-tier model. Each hub (Marketing, Sales, Service, Content) has three paid tiers:

- **Starter**: €15/user/month — basic features only
- **Professional**: $90–$800+/month — unlocks automation, custom reporting, and sequences
- **Enterprise**: $150+/seat/month — required for audit logs, custom objects, and advanced permissions

For a team of 10 users needing automation and reporting, HubSpot Professional easily costs **$1,000–$1,500/month**. The same team on Customermates pays **€100/month** — with identical core CRM functionality, [workflow automation](/features/workflow-automation), [reporting](/features/reporting), and AI agents included.

Over 12 months, that is a difference of **$10,800–$16,800** in savings. For startups and growing teams, that budget is better spent on actual growth.

With Customermates, you [sign up](/auth/signup) and start working in two minutes. ## Core CRM Features Compared

Both Customermates and HubSpot cover the CRM essentials: [contact and organization management](/features/contact-management), [deal tracking with pipelines](/features/pipeline), [task management](/features/task-management), [email integration](/features/email-integration), and team collaboration. The fundamental difference lies in philosophy and access.

### Contact and Deal Management

Customermates offers unlimited contacts, organizations, deals, and custom fields on every plan. HubSpot also provides unlimited contacts on its free tier, but advanced contact scoring, calculated properties, and custom objects require Professional or Enterprise plans.

Both platforms support [pipeline views](/features/pipeline) including list, Kanban, and board layouts. Customermates includes all view types by default; HubSpot restricts some pipeline features to paid tiers.

### Reporting and Analytics

Customermates includes [reporting and analytics](/features/reporting) in the base plan. You can build custom dashboards, track deal velocity, and analyze team performance without any upgrade.

HubSpot limits free and Starter users to basic, pre-built reports. Custom report builders, advanced analytics, and revenue attribution require the Professional tier ($90+/month). For data-driven teams, this is a significant limitation.

### AI Agents

Customermates includes AI agents that help your team prioritize [leads](/features/lead-management), draft follow-up messages, and automate routine decisions. These are available to every user at no additional cost.

HubSpot offers AI features under its "Breeze" brand, but many advanced AI capabilities — such as predictive lead scoring and AI-powered content recommendations — are locked behind higher-tier plans or sold as paid add-ons.

## Automation and Integration

### Workflow Automation

Customermates integrates natively with [n8n](https://n8n.io/), the leading open-source automation engine. This gives you access to 400+ integrations from day one: sync contacts with your accounting software, trigger [Slack notifications](/features/slack-integration) on deal updates, connect your [email](/features/email-integration), or build custom [reporting dashboards](/features/reporting). HubSpot offers an extensive marketplace with hundreds of integrations. However, the most powerful automation features — custom workflow actions, advanced sequences, and higher API rate limits — are tied to Professional and Enterprise plans. Building the integrations you actually need often requires a plan upgrade.

### Sales Automation

[Sales automation](/features/sales-automation) in Customermates is designed for teams that want to move fast without configuring complex rule trees. Automated follow-ups, deal stage transitions, and task assignment work out of the box.

HubSpot's sales automation is powerful at the enterprise level, but its sequences, playbooks, and forecasting tools are gated behind Sales Hub Professional ($90/seat/month) or Enterprise ($150/seat/month).

## Data Protection and GDPR Compliance

For European businesses, data protection is not a checkbox — it is a core requirement under GDPR. This is where a HubSpot alternative built for Europe makes the biggest difference.

Customermates is **GDPR-native** and hosts exclusively in the EU (Germany). Every feature — from data processing to backups — runs on European infrastructure. You can also [self-host](/features/self-hosted) the entire platform for complete data sovereignty, or use the managed [cloud CRM](/features/cloud-crm).

HubSpot stores data primarily in the United States. While HubSpot has worked toward GDPR compliance, its fundamental architecture relies on US-based data centers and subprocessors. True EU-only data residency is not guaranteed for all plan levels. For companies in regulated industries — finance, healthcare, legal — or those handling sensitive customer data under GDPR, this creates ongoing compliance risk that requires extra legal review and Data Processing Agreements.

## Open-Source Advantage

Customermates is fully open-source. This means:

- **Transparency**: You can inspect every line of code that handles your data
- **No vendor lock-in**: Export your data or self-host at any time
- **Community-driven development**: Feature requests and bug fixes from a growing community
- **Custom extensions**: Build on top of the platform without waiting for vendor roadmaps

HubSpot is proprietary software. You cannot audit the code, you cannot self-host, and migrating away requires exporting data through limited export tools. Your CRM data lives entirely within HubSpot's ecosystem.

For teams that value long-term flexibility and data ownership, open-source is not just a technical choice — it is a business strategy.
