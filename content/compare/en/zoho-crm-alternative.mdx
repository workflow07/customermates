## Why Teams Look for a Zoho Alternative

Zoho CRM is one of the most widely used CRM platforms globally, offering modules for sales, marketing, service, and more. Yet many teams run into the same frustrations after a few months:

- **Overwhelming complexity.** Zoho packs dozens of modules, submenus, and configuration layers into a single interface. Finding the right feature requires navigating a labyrinth of settings. Even experienced users report spending too much time on administration rather than selling.
- **Tiered feature restrictions.** Workflow rules, custom fields, reports, API access, and storage are all capped based on your plan. The free plan allows only one workflow rule and five custom views per module. Even the Standard plan keeps many critical features restricted.
- **Hidden costs.** Premium support, additional storage, data backups, and portals for external users are paid add-ons. What appears affordable at sign-up frequently doubles once a team needs production-grade functionality.
- **Non-EU default hosting.** Zoho processes data globally, primarily in the USA and India. While EU data centres exist, they are not the default. For GDPR-conscious businesses, this creates compliance headaches.
- **AI locked behind expensive tiers.** Zoho's AI assistant Zia is only available from the Enterprise plan at €40/user/month, putting intelligent automation out of reach for smaller teams.

If these pain points sound familiar, it is worth evaluating a Zoho alternative that removes these barriers entirely.
