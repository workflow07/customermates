> [Pipedrive](https://www.pipedrive.com/) is a well-known, sales-focused CRM built around visual pipeline management. It has earned a reputation for being more approachable than enterprise platforms like Salesforce. However, its strict record limits, plan-gated features, and costly add-ons make it expensive as teams grow. Customermates is an open-source Pipedrive alternative with flat pricing, no record limits, EU hosting, and full automation included from day one.

## Why Switch from Pipedrive?

Teams switch from Pipedrive for several recurring reasons. The most common complaints include hitting record limits on lower plans, paying for add-ons that should be core features, limited automation unless you upgrade, and concerns about data hosting outside the EU. If any of these sound familiar, Customermates addresses each one directly.

Customermates is built on a different philosophy: every feature is available at a single flat price. There are no artificial caps on records, no plan tiers to navigate, and no add-on upsells. The [n8n automation engine](/features/workflow-automation) is included from the start, and all data stays in the EU.

## Setup and Onboarding

Customermates lets you get started in two minutes. Sign up, configure your [deal pipelines](/features/pipeline), import your [contacts](/features/contact-management), and begin working immediately. The interface is designed to be understood without training or onboarding sessions.

Pipedrive is considered user-friendly compared to enterprise CRMs, but onboarding still requires meaningful time investment. The system is tightly built around its sales pipeline model. Teams with workflows that extend beyond a classic B2B sales process often find themselves adapting to Pipedrive's structure rather than the other way around. Users frequently report that the learning curve is steeper than expected, particularly when configuring automations and custom fields.

## Pricing: Flat Rate vs. Tiered Plans

The pricing difference between Customermates and Pipedrive is substantial. Customermates costs **€10 per user per month** with all features included, no tiers, and no hidden caps. See our [pricing page](/pricing) for full details.

[Pipedrive's pricing](https://www.pipedrive.com/en/pricing) has four tiers (as of 2026):

- **Lite**: €14/user/month (annual) or €24/user/month (monthly)
- **Growth**: €39/user/month (annual) or €49/user/month (monthly)
- **Premium**: €49/user/month (annual) or €79/user/month (monthly)
- **Ultimate**: €79/user/month (annual) or €99/user/month (monthly)

Since you cannot mix plan types within one account, every user must be on the same tier. A team of five on the Premium plan pays €245/month (annual billing) or €395/month (monthly billing).

On top of plan costs, Pipedrive offers paid add-ons: LeadBooster (from €32.50/month), Web Visitors (€41/month), Campaigns (from €13.33/month), and Projects (€6.70/user/month). These additional costs accumulate fast.

Customermates imposes no record limits. Unlimited contacts, unlimited deals, unlimited custom fields, all at the base price. ## Data Protection and GDPR Compliance

Customermates hosts exclusively in the EU (Germany) and is GDPR-native by design. [Self-hosting](/features/self-hosted) is available for organizations requiring complete data sovereignty. Every data processing decision is made with European data protection standards as the baseline, not as an afterthought.

Pipedrive claims GDPR compliance and hosts data in both the EU (Frankfurt, Dublin) and the USA (AWS). EU customers contract with Pipedrive's Estonian entity, but the company uses US-based subprocessors, and data may transfer between regions. For businesses with strict data residency requirements, particularly in finance, healthcare, or public sector, this mixed hosting model creates compliance uncertainty.

For teams where GDPR compliance is non-negotiable, Customermates removes the ambiguity entirely.

Whether you need [Slack integration](/features/slack-integration), [email synchronization](/features/email-integration), or custom [CRM integrations](/features/integrations), everything works without upgrade requirements.

Pipedrive offers a marketplace with 500+ integrations and solid API access. However, workflow automation is only available from the Growth plan (€39/user/month), and many automation features require Premium or higher. The result is that the integrations you need most often require a plan upgrade to fully utilize.
