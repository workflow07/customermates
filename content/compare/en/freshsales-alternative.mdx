Teams move away from Freshsales for several recurring reasons. The free plan is heavily restricted and serves primarily as a lead-generation tool. Paid plans gate critical features behind expensive tiers: API access requires Pro ($39/user/month), audit logs require Pro, and advanced workflow automation is limited on lower plans. Freddy AI bot sessions are capped and billed as add-ons.

Customermates takes a different approach. Every feature is available at a single flat price of €10 per user per month. The [n8n automation engine](/features/workflow-automation) is included from the start, [API access](/features/api) has no plan restrictions, and AI agents help manage [lead tracking](/features/lead-tracking) and follow-up workflows without session limits.

## Setup and Onboarding

Customermates lets you get started in two minutes. Sign up, configure your [deal pipelines](/features/pipeline), import your [contacts](/features/contact-management), and begin working immediately. The interface is designed to be understood without training or onboarding sessions.

Freshsales offers a 21-day free trial of its full feature set, which sounds generous but creates a problem: teams configure workflows and integrations during the trial, only to lose access to many of those features when the trial ends and they choose a lower-priced plan. The actual onboarding for a production setup typically takes hours to days, depending on how many Freshworks ecosystem products you want to connect.

The pricing difference between Customermates and Freshsales is significant. Customermates costs **€10 per user per month** with all features included. See our [pricing page](/pricing) for full details.

[Freshsales pricing](https://www.freshworks.com/crm/pricing/) has four paid tiers (as of 2026):

The Growth plan appears affordable but is heavily restricted: no API access, limited workflows (5 sequences), no audit logs, no territory management, and limited custom reports. To work productively, most teams need at least the Pro plan at $39/user/month.

## Data Protection and GDPR Compliance

Customermates hosts exclusively in the EU (Germany) and is GDPR-native by design. [Self-hosting](/features/self-hosted) is available for organizations requiring complete data sovereignty. Every data processing decision is made with European data protection standards as the baseline.

Freshsales is headquartered in India with primary data centers in India and the United States. EU servers are available but not the default configuration. Freshworks uses global subprocessors, which means customer data may traverse non-EU jurisdictions. For businesses subject to strict European data protection requirements, particularly in finance, healthcare, or public sector, this creates compliance complexity.
