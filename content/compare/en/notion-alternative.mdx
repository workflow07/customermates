## Why Teams Search for a Notion Alternative

Notion is a powerful workspace for documentation, wikis, and lightweight project management. But when teams try to use it as a CRM, they quickly hit walls:

- **No native pipelines.** You must build Kanban boards from scratch and manually move cards between stages, there is no deal pipeline with stage automation, weighted values, or win-rate tracking.
- **No email integration.** Notion cannot sync with Gmail or Outlook. Every customer email must be copied and pasted manually.
- **No CRM reporting.** There are no built-in sales dashboards, [pipeline reports](/features/reporting), or forecasting tools.
- **No CRM-specific automation.** Notion's basic automations handle database triggers and notifications, but they cannot automate [follow-ups](/features/follow-up), reassign leads, or move deals through stages based on activity.
- **US-hosted data.** Notion stores data on AWS in the United States. For European teams handling customer data under GDPR, this creates compliance risk.

These are not edge cases, they are the daily requirements of any team managing customer relationships. A real [CRM system](/blog/crm-system) solves all of them out of the box.
