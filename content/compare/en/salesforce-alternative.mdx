> Salesforce is the undisputed market leader in CRM -- a platform that has grown over decades and was built for enterprise companies with extensive requirements. That makes it incredibly powerful, but also costly, complex, and demanding to implement. Customermates takes the opposite approach: simplicity, speed, and transparency -- ideal for modern teams that want to start immediately without wading through licensing models, consultants, and months-long onboarding.

## Setup & Onboarding

With Customermates, you are ready to go in two minutes. The interface is self-explanatory, intuitive, and immediately usable without training. You can work directly with real customer data without spending days on configuration.

Salesforce is not designed for quick starts. Even the entry-level Starter Suite requires configuration, training, and often external support. The platform follows enterprise logic with lifecycle stages, complex workflows, object relationships, and a steep learning curve. What Salesforce markets as a quick start often translates to weeks or months in practice before the system is truly productive.

## Pricing & Cost Model

Customermates costs a flat €10 per user per month -- all features included, no hidden costs.

Salesforce restructured its pricing into five tiers: Starter Suite at $25/user/month, Pro Suite at $100/user/month, Enterprise at $165/user/month, Unlimited at $330/user/month, and Einstein 1 Sales at $500/user/month. Additional costs for add-ons like Einstein AI ($50/user/month extra), Revenue Intelligence, CPQ, or industry-specific clouds accumulate rapidly.

A team of 10 salespeople on the Pro Suite pays $1,000/month -- and that is before any add-ons. The same team on Customermates pays €100/month total with every feature included. For teams that need CRM rather than an enterprise platform, Salesforce is disproportionately expensive.

## Open-Source & Transparency

Customermates is open-source. You can inspect the code, self-host the system, and maintain full control over your data and infrastructure. There is no vendor lock-in and no opaque licensing.

Salesforce is proprietary software with one of the most complex licensing models in the industry. Switching away from Salesforce is notoriously difficult and expensive, with data migration, AppExchange dependency, and custom development all creating lock-in.

## Usage Limits & Flexibility

Salesforce enforces storage limits, API call limits, and custom field limits that vary by edition. The Starter Suite is limited to basic CRM functions, and scaling to more advanced features requires expensive upgrades. Custom objects, validation rules, and workflow automations are restricted in lower tiers.

Customermates imposes no artificial limits. Unlimited records, unlimited custom fields, unlimited pipelines -- at a consistent, fair price per user.

## AI Agents & Automation

Salesforce offers AI through Einstein, but it comes at a premium. Einstein AI features require the more expensive editions or paid add-ons. Agentforce, Salesforce's native AI integration platform, adds yet another cost layer.

Customermates natively integrates with native AI integration natively. Combined with built-in n8n integration, you can build intelligent, automated workflows from day one -- without paying extra for AI capabilities or hiring consultants to configure them.

## Data Protection & Hosting

Customermates hosts exclusively in the EU (Germany) and fully complies with GDPR. Self-hosting is available for organizations that require complete data sovereignty.

Salesforce operates a global cloud infrastructure with data processing that can occur outside the EU depending on configuration. While Salesforce offers EU data residency options, they require specific contractual arrangements and do not cover all services equally.

## Integrations

Both systems offer API access and integrations. Salesforce has a massive AppExchange with thousands of integrations -- but many are paid, edition-dependent, or complex to implement. The platform's integration power comes with consultant-level complexity.

Customermates relies on n8n integration, enabling flexible, self-configurable workflows that connect to thousands of services without additional licenses. The API is fully documented and available at the base price.

## Which System Is Right for You?

**Salesforce is right for you if:**
- You are an enterprise company with 100+ employees
- You have complex, global sales structures requiring multi-currency and territory management
- You have dedicated Salesforce admins and budget for certified consultants
- You need deep integration with the Salesforce ecosystem (Marketing Cloud, Service Cloud, Commerce Cloud)
- You have budget for $165+/user/month plus implementation costs

**Customermates is right for you if:**
- You are a modern team, startup, or growing business
- You want to work productively immediately without weeks of setup
- You want transparent pricing at €10/user/month with no hidden costs
- You value open-source software and data sovereignty
- You need GDPR compliance with EU hosting
- You want native AI integration and n8n automation included from day one
