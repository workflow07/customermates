## Automatic Data Collection vs. Conscious Control

Salesflare's core value proposition is automatic data entry. The system extracts contact information from emails, enriches profiles from LinkedIn, logs meetings from your calendar, and suggests follow-ups automatically. This sounds powerful but comes with significant trade-offs.

First, automation requires giving Salesflare access to your email accounts, calendars, and social networks. Your communication data is actively scanned and processed. For teams with strict data handling policies, this is a concern.

Second, automatic data recognition is imperfect. Contacts can be incorrectly assigned, meetings misinterpreted, and follow-up suggestions may not match your actual sales process. The system requires ongoing monitoring and correction.

Third, you lose control over what enters your CRM. Salesflare pulls in contacts from every email thread, including spam, newsletters, and one-time conversations. Maintaining a clean database requires regular cleanup.

Customermates prioritizes conscious data control. You decide which contacts, deals, and interactions enter the system. Combined with [AI agents](/features/lead-tracking) that help categorize and prioritize leads, you get intelligent assistance without sacrificing data sovereignty.
