Now part of Exact (acquired in 2023), weclapp serves businesses that need integrated ERP and CRM. However, for teams that primarily need CRM, weclapp's ERP-first approach means paying for and navigating complexity you do not need. Customermates provides focused, open-source CRM at a fraction of the price, with the same German hosting standards plus AI agents and n8n automation.

## Setup & Onboarding

weclapp is an ERP system with CRM features, not a CRM with optional ERP. Setting up weclapp means configuring the entire business suite: chart of accounts, tax settings, warehouse management, and CRM modules together. Even if you only need CRM, the system's ERP backbone adds setup complexity. Most businesses need days to weeks for a proper implementation, and many rely on weclapp partners for guided onboarding.

Customermates is ready in two minutes. Set up your pipelines, import contacts, and start tracking deals. No accounting configuration, no warehouse settings, no ERP overhead.

## Pricing & Cost Model

weclapp pricing is module-based and starts at approximately €39/user/month for the CRM package. The full ERP suite (ERP Starter, ERP Dienstleistung, ERP Handel) costs significantly more, and many CRM-relevant features are tied to higher-tier or combined packages. Since the Exact acquisition, pricing structures have been consolidating, and individual module pricing requires contacting sales.

For teams that only need CRM, paying €39+/user/month for an ERP system's CRM module is significantly more expensive than necessary.

Customermates costs €10/user/month. All CRM features are included. No modules to select, no ERP components to pay for, no evolving pricing structures. A team of 10 pays €100/month with Customermates vs. €390+/month with weclapp's CRM module.

## Open-Source & Independence

Customermates is open-source. You can inspect the code, self-host the platform, and maintain full ownership of your data and infrastructure. There is no vendor dependency.

weclapp is proprietary software, now owned by Exact (a Dutch ERP company). This acquisition introduces uncertainty about the product's long-term roadmap, pricing evolution, and strategic direction. Your data lives on their infrastructure, and the migration path if weclapp's direction changes is through Exact's ecosystem, not on your own terms.

Customermates gives you independence: open-source code, self-hosting capability, and no corporate acquisition risk affecting your CRM.

## Feature Comparison

weclapp's CRM module covers the essentials: contacts, organizations, opportunities, quotes, and campaign management. Its strength lies in the connection to ERP, when you need CRM data to flow directly into invoicing, inventory, and purchasing processes, weclapp's integrated approach has clear value.

However, as a CRM tool in isolation, weclapp's capabilities are limited compared to dedicated CRM platforms. Pipeline management is functional but basic. The user interface prioritizes ERP workflows and can feel cumbersome for pure CRM usage. Mobile optimization is limited, and the overall user experience reflects ERP software conventions rather than modern CRM design.

Customermates delivers focused CRM: contacts, organizations, deals, pipelines, tasks, services, email integration, AI agents, and n8n automation. Every feature is designed for CRM workflows, not adapted from an ERP context. The interface is modern, responsive, and built for how sales and service teams actually work.
