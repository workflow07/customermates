> [Salesmate](https://www.salesmate.io/) is a feature-rich CRM for small and medium-sized businesses with a strong focus on sales, built-in calling, and email sequences. It packs many capabilities into its platform, but distributes them across four pricing tiers with aggressive upselling. What starts at $23 per user quickly becomes $40-63 when you need the features that actually matter. On top of that, built-in calling incurs separate charges for phone numbers, SMS, and call credits. Customermates is an open-source Salesmate alternative with flat pricing, all features included, EU hosting, and no hidden telephony fees.

## Why Switch from Salesmate?

Teams move away from Salesmate for several recurring reasons. The Starter plan ($23/user/month) is missing product management, email sequences, and advanced permission controls. The AI assistant Sandy is locked behind the Boost plan ($40/user/month). Custom layouts are Enterprise-only ($63/user/month). And built-in calling, one of Salesmate's headline features, adds unpredictable costs through phone number rentals, SMS credits, and per-minute call charges.

Customermates takes a different approach. Every feature is available at a single flat price of €10 per user per month. The [n8n automation engine](/features/workflow-automation) is included from the start, native AI integration help manage [lead tracking](/features/lead-tracking) and follow-ups, and you are never forced into a proprietary telephony system.

Customermates lets you get started in two minutes. Sign up, configure your [deal pipelines](/features/pipeline), import your [contacts](/features/contact-management), and begin working immediately. The interface is clean and requires no training.

Salesmate does not require weeks of implementation, but setup still takes 1-2 days. ## Pricing: Flat Rate vs. Tiered Plans

The pricing difference between Customermates and Salesmate is significant. Customermates costs **€10 per user per month** with all features included. See our [pricing page](/pricing) for full details.

- **Starter**: $23/user/month (annual) or $29/user/month (monthly)

- **Enterprise**: $63/user/month (annual) or custom pricing

Many essential features are plan-gated:
- **Product management & email sequences**: Growth plan and above
- **Sandy AI assistant**: Boost plan and above
- **Custom layouts & validation rules**: Enterprise only
- **Permission management**: Growth plan and above
